About Me

My name is Matthew (Matt) Richards and I am a data science fellow at Insight Data Science. This prediction model and web application were part of my project at Insight during the Winter 2018 session.

Prior to joining Insight, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow at the Institute for Systems Biology. As a member of the Hood-Price Lab, my work was focused on building tools and resources to better understand how genes interact with one another.
